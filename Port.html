Who am I?

  • Who is Max Kruger?

    • Welcome to my website! I am Max Kruger. I study at The University of Witwatersrand in South Africa. I am currently a full-time student studying Digital Arts. I have a bunch of hobbies and interests all in the field of Gaming and Game Development. Check out some of my work below! I love making games in my free time, both in Game Jams and when I just feel like it. My passion is in FPS games since this is the genre I grew up with and I still love to this day! Games such as Counter Strike, Call Of Duty and Battlefield have always been staples in my play time. One day I decided to try actually recreate some of the system we see in these games and it all of a sudden making games become something I love!
